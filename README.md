# Green Delivery Experiment (oTree)

This project implements a behavioural experiment to study seller competition and environmental decisions in e-commerce logistics.

## Overview

Participants act as competing manufacturers and make decisions on:

- Production quantity (q)
- Emission level (g)

The experiment consists of 10 rounds:

- Rounds 1–5: Low carbon price (k = 2)
- Rounds 6–10: High carbon price (k = 10)

## Objective

To examine how carbon pricing influences:

- Emission reduction behaviour
- Strategic interaction under competition

## Structure

- `green_game/` – main experimental app
- `settings.py` – experiment configuration
- `requirements.txt` – dependencies
- `Procfile` – deployment setup (Railway)
